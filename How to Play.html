HOW TO PLAY

When you are ready to play, click the 'DRAW' button.

Once you click the 'DRAW' button, you will be given a prompt to draw (top left of drawing window) and the timer will begin.

Click on the colors to change your brush color. If you make a mistake, you cna click the undo arrow.

Once the timer runs out, your drawing will be posted for others to see.
